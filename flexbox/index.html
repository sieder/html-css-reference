Flexbox

  • The ability to alter item width and height to best fit in its contatiners available free space
  • Flexbox is direction-agnostic
  • Builtt for small-scale layouts while the upcoming Grid specification is for more large scale

Flex Properties

    display: flex | inline-flex;

    flex-direction: row | column

    flex-wrap: wrap | nowrap | wrapreverse

    flex-basis: <length>

    justify-content: flex-start | flex-end | center

    align-self: flex-start | flex-end | center

    align-items: flex-start | flex-end | center

    align-content: flex-start | flex-end | center

    flex-grow: <number>

    flex-shrink: <number>

    flex: <integer>

    order: <integer>
